Delete your WhapVibez account

WhapVibez, provided by Nexvibe Limited, allows you to request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Delete your account in the app

  1. Open WhapVibez and sign in.
  2. Open Profile, then Settings.
  3. Select Manage account and then Delete account.
  4. Follow the confirmation steps shown in the app.

Request deletion without the app

Email us from the address associated with your WhapVibez account. Include the username or email address of the account you want deleted.

privacy@whapvibez.com
Subject: WhapVibez account deletion request

Data that is deleted

  • Your profile and account identifiers.
  • Your posts, uploaded media, messages, community activity, and shop information where applicable.
  • Other personal data associated with the account, except information we must retain for legal, fraud-prevention, security, tax, or financial-record obligations.

Processing time

We verify and process email deletion requests as soon as reasonably possible. Data retained for a legal obligation is isolated and deleted when that obligation ends.

For more information, read our Privacy Policy.
